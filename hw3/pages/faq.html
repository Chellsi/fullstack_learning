What documents do I need for international travel?

Requirements vary by country, but generally, you may need:
    
            A passport (make sure it’s valid for at least six months).
            A visa (check the entry requirements for your destination).
            Travel insurance (mandatory for some countries).
            Visit official embassy websites to check current regulations.

How can I save money while traveling?

Here are a few budget-friendly travel tips:
    
            Book flights and accommodation in advance.
            Use discount services and loyalty programs.
            Travel during the off-season.
            Choose budget airlines and accommodations.
